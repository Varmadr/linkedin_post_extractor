🚀 Adya.ai Launch Lab Inaugurated at KIT – Empowering the Next Generation of AI Innovators

Kalaignarkarunanidhi Institute of Technology (KIT), Coimbatore proudly inaugurated the Adya.ai Launch Lab, marking an important milestone in strengthening the institution’s commitment to cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence innovation and industry collaboration ,the lab has been inaugurated on March 7,2026

The launch was graced by Shayak Mazumder, Founder & CEO of Adya.ai, a pioneering AI platform company focused on building next-generation agentic AI solutions and intelligent automation systems

The Adya.ai Launch Lab at KIT will serve as a vibrant hub for students to explore advanced AI technologies, develop real-world AI applications, and engage in hands-on innovation using modern AI tools and platforms. This initiative aims to nurture future AI leaders by bridging academia with industry-driven AI research and product development.

Through this collaboration, students will gain exposure to:
🔹 Agentic AI and Generative AI technologies
🔹 Building real-world AI products and applications
🔹 Industry mentorship and startup innovation
🔹 Research-driven learning aligned with global AI trends

We extend our sincere gratitude to Shayak Mazumder and the Adya.ai team for partnering with KIT in this transformative initiative that will empower students to innovate, experiment, and lead in the rapidly evolving AI ecosystem.

✨ A new chapter begins at KIT as we continue to build a strong ecosystem for AI innovation, industry collaboration, and future-ready talent development.
